Wind Simple v2.0

Quick Anchor is an easy-to-use computer program
to calculate the tensile and shear strengths of cast-in-place
and post-installed anchors (single or a group) in accordance
with the provisions of ACI 318.
Through a simple and intuitive interface, it only takes a few minutes
to input all the necessary information for the program
to calculate the strengths of a single anchor or a group of anchors
in four tensile failure modes and three shear failure modes
as well as splitting, as specified in ACI 318.
